# Pricing Guide - Vedabase Original Edition

## ePub Pricing

**All ePubs: $0.99 USD**

- At $0.99, Amazon pays 35% royalty = **$0.35 per sale**
- Volume strategy: low price = maximum reach
- Goal: spread the message, not maximize profit

---

## Print Book Pricing (KDP)

### KDP Print Cost Formula

```
Printing Cost = (Fixed Cost) + (Page Count × Per-Page Cost)

For 6x9 Black & White, Cream Paper:
- Fixed cost: $0.85
- Per-page cost: $0.012

Example: 200-page book
Cost = $0.85 + (200 × $0.012) = $0.85 + $2.40 = $3.25
```

### Pricing for 30% Royalty

KDP gives 60% royalty on print books (list price - print cost).

**Formula for 30% profit margin:**
```
List Price = Print Cost / 0.70

(This gives 30% of list price as profit after Amazon's 40% cut)
```

## Strategy Notes

1. **Low prices = maximum distribution** - Goal is spreading the original texts
2. **Bundle opportunity** - Create box sets at slight discount
3. **ePub at $0.99** - Impulse purchase, low barrier
4. **Print near minimum** - Affordable physical copies
5. **Consider "free" promotions** - KDP Select allows 5 free days/quarter
