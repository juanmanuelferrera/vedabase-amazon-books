## Typography

### Primary (Covers & Headers)
- **Font:** Adobe Caslon Pro or Garamond Premier Pro
- **Weight:** Regular for body, Bold for titles
- **Style:** Classic, timeless, scholarly

### Secondary (Interior)
- **Body:** 11pt Garamond
- **Headers:** Optima or similar sans-serif
- **Sanskrit:** Devanagari when needed
