# About This Edition

This text has been meticulously transcribed from high-resolution scans of the original first edition, published during the author's lifetime and under his direct supervision.

**No posthumous editorial changes have been made.**

Every word, every phrase, every Sanskrit transliteration appears exactly as Srila Prabhupada approved it. This is not a "revised" or "updated" edition—it is the original, preserved.

## Why This Matters

After the author's departure in 1977, thousands of changes were introduced to his books by subsequent editors. Words were altered, phrases restructured, and meanings subtly shifted—all without the author's consent or knowledge.

This edition restores what was taken: the exact words the author chose to transmit his message.
