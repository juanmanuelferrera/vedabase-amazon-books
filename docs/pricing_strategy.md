## Pricing Psychology

- **Under $10**: Impulse buy zone
- **$7.99**: Sweet spot for small-medium books
- **$2.99 ebook**: 70% royalty threshold (vs 35% below)

## Competitive Context

- BBT editions: $12-18 (paperback)
- Our advantage: Lower price + "original unedited" positioning
- Message: "Less than a lunch, original words"

## KDP Minimum Prices

Based on 6x9 black & white, cream paper:
- Under 100 pages: ~$3.50 minimum
- 100-200 pages: ~$4.50 minimum
- 200-300 pages: ~$5.50 minimum
- 300-400 pages: ~$6.50 minimum

Always verify in KDP calculator before publishing.
